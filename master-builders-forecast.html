Master Builders sits on an exceptionally valuable dataset — real-time construction project flows, subcontractor performance, payment behaviours, and supplier pricing across 52,000+ builders. Below are structured monetisation pathways from near-term (Year 1) through to long-term strategic plays.

Year 1

📊 Benchmark Reports & Analytics

Anonymised industry benchmarks — average project margins, subcontractor rates by trade/region, payment performance scores. Sold as subscription reports to builders, developers, and lenders.

Target Customers: Builders (in-app), Property developers, Banks
$50–$200/mo per subscriber
Est. Y2 potential: $2M–$8M ARR at 5% builder adoption
Year 1

⭐ Subcontractor Credit Scoring

Payment reliability scores derived from platform payment data — equivalent to a credit bureau for the trades industry. Licensed to lenders, insurers, and hiring platforms.

Target Customers: Trade insurers, Equipment finance, Banks
$0.50–$2.00 per API query
Est. Y2 potential: $1M–$5M at scale
Year 2

🏦 Lending Insights for Finance Partners

Real project cash flow data fed into lending decisions — enabling revenue-based or invoice financing for builders and trades. Master Builders earns referral/data fees from finance partners.

Target Customers: Fintechs, Non-bank lenders, BNPL for trades
0.5%–1.5% of loan originated
Est. Y3 potential: $10M–$40M at scale
Year 2

🏗️ Materials Pricing Intelligence

Aggregated supplier quotes and purchase orders create a real-time materials price index for Australian construction. Sold as a data feed to suppliers, cost estimators, and developers.

Target Customers: Quantity surveyors, Developers, Suppliers
$500–$5,000/mo enterprise feed
Est. Y3 potential: $3M–$15M ARR
Year 2

🎯 Targeted Advertising & Lead Gen

Suppliers and product manufacturers pay for targeted placement inside the app — reaching builders at the point of procurement. High-intent, context-aware advertising.

Target Customers: Building material brands, Tool companies, Software vendors
CPM $40–$120 / CPC $8–$30
Est. Y2 potential: $2M–$8M
Year 3

📍 Regional Construction Activity Index

Live construction starts, project volumes, and payment velocity by suburb/LGA. Sold as a planning intelligence tool to councils, REITs, and infrastructure investors.

Target Customers: Councils, REITs, Infrastructure funds, PropTech
$10,000–$100,000/yr enterprise
Est. Y3 potential: $5M–$20M ARR
Year 3

🤖 AI-Powered Project Risk Scoring

ML models trained on project completion patterns, payment delays, and dispute data — surface risk scores to lenders, insurers, and developers before they commit capital.

Target Customers: Banks, Insurers, Developers, Surveyors
SaaS $2,000–$20,000/mo
Est. Y3+ potential: $20M–$80M ARR
Strategic / Exit

📦 Data Licensing to Major Banks / ABS

At scale, Master Builders holds what no public dataset has — granular, real-time construction economic activity. Strategic licensing to the RBA, ABS, ANZ, or CBA as a macro indicator.

Target Customers: RBA, ABS, Big 4 Banks, SuperFunds
$1M–$10M+ per deal
Significant uplift to valuation / acquisition multiple
